Jean Paul Gaultier: Gaultier Divine Intense

₱7,500.00

Jean Paul Gaultier: Gaultier Divine Intense is a radiant floral marine fragrance for women, launched in 2023. This scent celebrates femininity in all its glory, embodying a powerful and dazzling goddess.

It's a bold and captivating interpretation of the original Divine, with an even more opulent and solar presence. The fragrance beautifully balances a salty aquatic note with luscious floral and sweet undertones, creating an unforgettable aroma.

Top note: Calypsone (a marine note)

Middle notes: Lily, Ylang-Ylang, Jasmine

Base notes: Meringue, Patchouli

Gaultier Divine Intense is perfect for making a grand entrance and leaving a luminous, memorable impression, ideal for both day and evening wear.
