Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium

₱8,000.00

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium is an addictive gourmand floral fragrance for women, launched in 2014. This captivating scent is a bold and intense reinterpretation of the classic Opium, designed for the modern, edgy woman.

It combines the thrill of coffee with the sweetness of vanilla and the brightness of white flowers, creating an intoxicating and energetic aroma.

Top notes: Pear, Pink Pepper, Orange Blossom

Middle notes: Coffee, Jasmine, Bitter Almond, Licorice

Base notes: Vanilla, Patchouli, Cedar, Cashmere Wood

Black Opium is a glamorous choice, perfect for evening wear and making a distinctive, memorable statement.
