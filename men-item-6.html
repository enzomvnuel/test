Emporio Armani Stronger With You Parfum

₱6,500.00

Stronger With You Parfum by Emporio Armani is a powerful and highly addictive amber fougère woody fragrance for men, launched in 2023. This intense and sophisticated scent reimagines the original Stronger With You DNA with an elevated and deeper masculinity, capturing the essence of unwavering connection and passionate love.

It opens with a fresh burst that quickly transitions into a rich, warm, and inviting heart, settling into a long-lasting and profoundly sensual base.

Top notes: Lavender, Black Pepper

Middle notes: Chestnut, Vanilla, Amberwood

Base notes: Guaiac Wood, Cashmeran

Stronger With You Parfum is an exceptional choice for cooler weather, evening wear, and special occasions where you want to make a lasting impression. Its intense longevity and potent sillage ensure you'll be noticed.
