Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male Elixir

₱7,000.00

Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male Elixir is an amber fougère fragrance for men, launched in 2023. This intense and addictive scent plunges you into the golden hour of the high seas, embodying a charismatic sailor with undeniable appeal.

It's a rich and captivating take on the iconic Le Male, featuring a blend of warm, sweet, and spicy notes that create an unforgettable aroma.

Top notes: Lavender, Mint

Middle notes: Vanilla, Benzoin

Base notes: Honey, Tonka Bean, Tobacco

Perfect for evening wear and making a lasting impression, Le Male Elixir is a truly luxurious and charming fragrance.
