Chanel No. 5

₱5,000.00

Chanel No. 5 is an iconic floral aldehyde fragrance for women, first launched in 1921. It's a timeless and elegant scent, celebrated worldwide for its complex and harmonious composition. This perfume embodies sophistication and enduring femininity.

It features a bouquet of flowers blended with aldehydes, giving it its unique sparkling quality.

Top notes: Aldehydes, Ylang-Ylang, Neroli, Bergamot, Peach

Middle notes: Iris, Jasmine, Rose, Lily-of-the-Valley

Base notes: Sandalwood, Vanilla, Oakmoss, Vetiver, Patchouli

Chanel No. 5 is a versatile fragrance suitable for any occasion, leaving a classic and memorable impression.
