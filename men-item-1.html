Bleu De Chanel

₱10,000.00

Bleu de Chanel is a woody aromatic fragrance for men, launched in 2010. It is a timeless and sophisticated scent, perfect for the modern gentleman.

Top notes: Grapefruit, Lemon, Mint, Pink Pepper

Middle notes: Ginger, Nutmeg, Jasmine, Iso E Super

Base notes: Incense, Vetiver, Cedar, Sandalwood, Patchouli, White Musk

This fragrance offers a captivating and versatile aroma that can be worn for any occasion, from casual daytime outings to elegant evening events.
